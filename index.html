What is the GrizzODB?

The GrizzODB is a website that collects records of all of the student organizations here at Oakland University and displays them in one place! This is an effective way for organization leadership to view all of their members. Students can also see important information that their organization wishes to communicate.

Registering!

Our organization page is where you can register yourself as a member of an org, or actually create one! Choose your choice there. You can also view a list of all of the organizations currently registered with GrizzODB. Check it out! New features will be added regularly.

About Us

This website was created for CIT252: Interactive Web Systems at Oakland University. Visit this page to learn about the creators of this website, who are all students in the class!
